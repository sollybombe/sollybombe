About Me

Hi, I’m Solly Bombe — a full-stack developer and system designer based in Pretoria, South Africa. I bridge frontend beauty with backend logic to craft seamless, responsive systems that solve real business challenges.

My stack includes Python, Flask, JavaScript, Tailwind CSS, SQLite, and modular UI frameworks. I have a particular affinity for glassmorphic layouts, clean grids, and subtle animations that elevate user experience. Whether it’s building internal tools, dashboards, or scalable platforms, I balance performance, clarity, and visual polish.

I'm always exploring ways to make technical systems more intuitive, secure, and expressive. From data visualizations to phishing simulation for cybersecurity awareness, I love combining code with meaning.

This portfolio is not just a gallery — it's a reflection of how I think, design, and build. Let’s turn ideas into clean, working products.
